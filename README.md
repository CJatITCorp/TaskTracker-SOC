# TaskTracker-SOC

A self-contained task dashboard for the Services Operations Coordinator role at ITCorp.

## What it does
- Tracks tasks across the 7 KPI pillars of the SOC role (Finance & Bookkeeping,
  Service Delivery & Ticketing, Internal Systems & Improvement, Marketing & Client
  Comms, AI & Technology Adoption, Business Acquisition Support, Director & Operations
  Support)
- Separate competency panel for the 8-segment bookkeeping assessment cycle
  (Bank Recons, Invoice Packs, Invoice Processing, Debtors Follow-Up, Statements,
  Netcash Debit Orders, Supplier Recons, Management Accounts & Reporting)
- Board (Kanban) and Table views, filterable by pillar, finance tag, and frequency
- Quick-add and bulk-paste task entry
- Runs entirely in the browser — single HTML file, no install, no server

## Status
Personal working tool — data is illustrative, updated as work progresses.
